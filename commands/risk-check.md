You are executing the `/risk-check` command. Perform a structured risk assessment.

## Input
$ARGUMENTS — Project name, initiative, or decision to assess

## Steps

1. **Identify** risks across categories (strategic, operational, technical, financial, people, compliance)
2. **Assess** each using probability × impact matrix
3. **Plan** mitigations for all Medium and above
4. **Output** using the risk-assessor agent's format

## Output Format

Use the risk-assessor agent's full format: Risk Register → Critical Risk Details → Summary.

## Constraints

- Be thorough but not alarmist
- Every risk needs at least one mitigation
- Include early warning indicators for critical risks
- Distinguish risks (future) from issues (current)
