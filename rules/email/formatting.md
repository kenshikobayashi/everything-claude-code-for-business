# Email Formatting Rules

## Subject Lines

- Include action prefix: [Action Required], [FYI], [Decision Needed], [Urgent]
- Keep under 60 characters
- Be specific: "Q1 Budget Approval — Due March 25" not "About the budget"
- For replies: Keep the original subject unless the topic has changed

## Body Structure

- First sentence: Why you're writing
- Body: Key information (bullets for 3+ items)
- Last paragraph: Clear ask (what, who, by when)
- Total length: Under 200 words for routine emails

## Recipients

- **To**: People who need to take action
- **CC**: People who need to be informed
- **BCC**: Use sparingly (large distribution lists, privacy protection)
- Never CC someone's manager as an implicit escalation — be direct instead

## Reply Behavior

- Reply to the original language of the thread
- Reply-all only if everyone needs to see your response
- If changing the topic, start a new thread
