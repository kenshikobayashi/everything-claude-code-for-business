# Risk Management Skill

## When to Use

When identifying, assessing, or mitigating risks for any project, decision, or initiative.

## Risk Register Format

| # | Risk | Category | Probability | Impact | Rating | Response | Owner | Trigger | Status |
|---|------|----------|------------|--------|--------|----------|-------|---------|--------|

## Categories

Strategic, Operational, Technical, Financial, People, Compliance, Reputational

## Response Strategies

- **Avoid**: Change the plan to eliminate the risk
- **Mitigate**: Reduce probability or impact
- **Transfer**: Shift to a third party (insurance, outsourcing, contracts)
- **Accept**: Acknowledge and monitor with a contingency plan

## Probability × Impact Matrix

| | Low (1) | Medium (2) | High (3) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **High (3)** | 3 Medium | 6 High | 9 Critical |
| **Medium (2)** | 2 Low | 4 Medium | 6 High |
| **Low (1)** | 1 Low | 2 Low | 3 Medium |

## Rules

- Every identified risk needs at least one response strategy
- Critical risks need both mitigation AND contingency plans
- Include early warning indicators for high-rated risks
- Review the risk register regularly (weekly for active projects)
- Distinguish risks (uncertain future events) from issues (current problems)
