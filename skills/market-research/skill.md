# Market Research Skill

## When to Use

When sizing a market, analyzing industry dynamics, or evaluating market entry opportunities.

## Frameworks

### TAM/SAM/SOM
- **TAM** (Total Addressable Market): Total global demand if 100% market share
- **SAM** (Serviceable Addressable Market): TAM filtered by geography, segment, capability
- **SOM** (Serviceable Obtainable Market): Realistic capture based on capacity and competition

### Approach: Top-Down vs. Bottom-Up
- **Top-down**: Industry report → filter to segment → apply assumptions
- **Bottom-up**: # of target customers × average revenue per customer
- Best practice: Calculate both and reconcile

### Porter's Five Forces
1. **Competitive Rivalry**: How intense is current competition?
2. **Supplier Power**: Can suppliers raise prices or reduce quality?
3. **Buyer Power**: Can customers demand lower prices?
4. **Threat of Substitutes**: How easily can customers switch to alternatives?
5. **Threat of New Entry**: How hard is it for new competitors to enter?

### Trend Analysis
- **Direction**: Growing, stable, or declining
- **Velocity**: How fast is change happening
- **Drivers**: What forces are causing the change
- **Inflection points**: When will trends accelerate or reverse

## Checklist

- [ ] Market size calculated with clear methodology
- [ ] Both top-down and bottom-up estimates where possible
- [ ] Sources cited with dates
- [ ] Assumptions explicitly stated
- [ ] Confidence levels assigned to estimates
